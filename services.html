Our Services

Digital Business Transformation

We help organizations (our partners) evolve to meet customers need and enable employees to be productive and efficient through deployment of innovative technology. Our seasoned and cross-functional teams support partners to navigate the challenging shift from legacy systems and ways of working to platforms and solutions that enable speedy and quality adaptation.

Data Analysis & Engineering

We improve connectivity across engineering, operations and governance teams and apply automation and machine learning to ensure systems are reliable, efficient and cost-effective. We are a team of seasoned data engineers that focuses on practical applications of data collection and analysis. We also focus on the applications and harvesting of big data.

Blockchain Technology

Schull Technologies is leading the effort to identify and implement practical, results-driven uses for blockchain in both private (e.g. oil and gas) and public (government) sector. As an important foundation for digital transformation, blockchain powerfully enables advanced data analytics, including machine learning and artificial intelligence. We bring to bear deep expertise and lessons learned across multiple industries, along with decades of public sector experience, on exploring the impact blockchain can have within organizations. Whether you are just beginning to investigate how it can be leveraged or are ready to get started, we can help through the following offerings:

Cloud Computing

We help organizations bring applications to market faster through the cloud and reduce risk with consistent, secure and reliable deployments through automation and self-service.

Business Intelligence & Analytics

We support our partners by providing end to end services and solutions across key data components such as sourcing and analysis to transform organizational data into intelligence; and deliver self-serviceable business intelligence by creating interactive, reporting and visualization dashboards.

Software Application & Engineering

We help our partners determine, develop and deploy the right software; and we develop market-facing applications across web and mobile using our proven, agile approach to help our partners quickly address customer wants and needs while forging more relevant and stronger experiences

Cyber Security

Schull Tech is a technology solutions company focused on securing Information Technology and Physical assets for federal civilian and defense and higher education clients. We specialize in Computer Network Defense, Information Assurance and Technical Surveillance Countermeasures.

Our Management framework is applied to delivery of all our solutions and helps improve the efficiency, operational capacity and security of our clients environment. Please take a moment and discover how our solutions can help your organization achieve your mission.
